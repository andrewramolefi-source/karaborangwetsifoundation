"Invisible Scars, Visible Strength"
By Karabo Rangwetsi

A powerful memoir and guide designed to provide hope for those navigating the journey of healing. The author, Karabo Rangwetsi shares her experience about a lifetime marked by trauma, abuse and near-death experiences. She pencils how faith, courage and perseverance transformed those scars into strength.

In this moving account, Karabo shares a journey that began with multiple childhood and early adulthood traumas, including sexual assault, physical abuse, emotional suffering and being assaulted and left for dead. Yet, instead of focusing on the pain, the memoir turns its gaze towards healing, demonstrating that it is possible to rise above the darkest chapters of life.
